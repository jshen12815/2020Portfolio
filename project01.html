01 - Juice

Starting a new telecomm revolution

It started with a simple question: You can pay for minutes as you use them—why can’t you do the same for data? Juice on Verizon, the first wireless service where you get all the data you need, but only pay for what you use. Juice puts the customer in total control of their data usage and budget—and rewards them in the process. Juice is the fuel for your life.
